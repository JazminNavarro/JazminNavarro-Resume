Ever since I was little, I have always been interested in the art world and in further developing a relationship with my creative side. What used to be just arts and crafts soon led to advanced art classes in high school, and before I knew it, I became a full time college student at the University of California, Riverside, pursuing my goal as an art major. While my first years in college were a bit challenging due to the 2020 Covid Pandemic, it has taught me how to adapt to sudden situations while continuing to keep a strong and positive attitude.

Alongside school, I have had several job experiences that have made me familiar with customer service and the certain skills needed to provide a great service to others. A lot of these skills include team effort, patience, responsibility, punctuality, and initiative. Again, while there were a few challenges concerning Covid as well as the disorganization of management in certain companies, these experiences have only helped me to grow as an employee and a person. The balancing of school and my job was another new challenge I had to experience, but it helped teach me how to stay organized and proficient in both environments.

While I am still uncertain of the path I want to follow in my career, whether that be as a future multimedia designer, or a dance choreographer, or even establish my own cake decorating business, I will continue to learn and grow through whatever challenges or new experiences I encounter.
